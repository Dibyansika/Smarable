What is Smarable?

Starting from teens to old agers, a whole lot of people are suffering from body pain now-a-days. Due to digitalization, working officials were already suffering from pain from sitting infront of screens all day. In this addition to this,pandemic has caused the youth and kids to sit across computers too. AS a result of which, many-a-people are suffering from pain, which can lead to diseases like spondilytes and more.

How will it work?

First of all, people need to acknowledge the pain and not just ignore it. According to medical professionals, hot water bags can be really helpful.But often, we don't have the time to just lay down for the water bags, or we have some or other woek to do. Due to this ,we have come up with W-mach.

Others ways it can be interpreted

W-mach is a waearable machinery- a jacket, that will give us relief from the bodypain. It can be worn anytime and anywhere.Additionally, the temperature can be managed by connecting the jacket with your phone via bluetooth.

Thought behind the project

The cause

Body pain has become one of the widespread problems across the globe in people of all ages. Staring from teens to working class to old agers, everyone is suffering from some or different kind of body pain due to various reasons.While working class officials suffer due to sitting infront of screens all day, oldies suffer becuse of their age. Even sportspersons require a little warmth after their long physical day.

But the show must go on!

But that does not at all mean that we cn just sit back or lie down all day.Truthfully,the show must go on! So, to keep the work up and going, all that is needed is a heat pad.But heat pads can be quite inconvinient and difficult to carry.We cannot just move around with it; which is why we have come up with Smarable. It is a wearable heat pad that you can wear anywhere and anytime.Its slim fit technology makes it easier to wear and avoids the fear of it looking uncomfortable.Additionally, the feature of handling the temperature through the phone makes it even easier to handle.

Need during those chilly winter days.

Not only during a body pain our body needs warmth,but also we need it during those cold winter days. Sometimes during extreme cold temperatures, even winter wears don't work. This is where projrctname will come in handy, as it will allow to raise the temperature as required.We also plan to implement this in other types of wear like jackets to make it fashionable as well
